Delete the mirror-cleanup workflow and rework `ci.yml` into a lean, project-agnostic baseline
that runs entirely on Nx's LOCAL cache and passes green with no remote cache present (SC2, SC3).

Purpose: SC2 (mirror-cleanup.yml deleted; ci.yml free of `./start-cache-server`, the
`nx reset`+reseed dance, `windows-selfcheck`, and `publish-mirror`) and SC3 (5-job local-cache-only

workflow file.
</objective>
